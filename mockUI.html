"Momentum aur Impulse mein kya fark hai? Dono same lagte hain."
Live Whiteboard ✨
Step 2 / 3
🎙 AI Explanation — Hindi
Rahul, bahut achha sawaal hai — yeh dono related hain lekin alag cheezein hain!

Momentum (p) ek object ki "motion ki quantity" hai. Sochlo ek heavy truck 100 km/h pe ja rahi hai vs ek cycle — truck ka momentum zyada hai, isliye rokna mushkil hai. Formula: p = m × v.

Ab dekho yahan — bat ball ko hit kar raha hai. Yeh force (F) thode time Δt ke liye ball pe lagti hai. Yahi Impulse (J) hai — J = F × Δt.

Aur sabse important point: Impulse = Momentum mein change hota hai. Isliye dono ka unit same hai — N·s. Lekin impulse ek action (bat ka hit) hai, momentum ek result (ball ki motion) hai! 💡
